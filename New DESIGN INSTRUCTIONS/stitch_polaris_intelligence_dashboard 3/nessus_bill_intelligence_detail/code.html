Commercial Impact Summary

Last Updated: Oct 12, 2023

Bill C-27 represents the most significant overhaul of Canadian federal privacy legislation in decades. For enterprise technology firms, data brokers, and consumer-facing organizations, the proposed Consumer Privacy Protection Act (CPPA) introduces stringent new consent requirements and algorithmic transparency mandates.

lightbulb

Materiality Note: The introduction of the Artificial Intelligence and Data Act (AIDA) within this omnibus bill creates immediate compliance unknowns for generative AI deployers, lacking clear regulatory definitions for "high-impact" systems.

Financial penalties for non-compliance are severe, capping at the higher of $25,000,000 or 5% of gross global revenue for the preceding financial year. Organizations must begin auditing data lineage and algorithmic decision-making models immediately to prepare for the transition period post-Royal Assent.
