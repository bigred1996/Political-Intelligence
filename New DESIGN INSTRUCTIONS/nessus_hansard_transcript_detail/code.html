Hon. Jonathan Wilkinson (Minister of Energy and Natural Resources, Lib.)
Opening Remarks

Mr. Speaker, I am pleased to rise today to discuss the implementation frameworks surrounding the Clean Electricity Regulations. We recognize that while the goal of a net-zero grid by 2035 is non-negotiable, the pathway there must account for regional realities.

Specifically, we are looking at extending the end-of-life provisions for certain natural gas generating units in provinces heavily reliant on fossil fuels, provided they incorporate carbon capture technology.
