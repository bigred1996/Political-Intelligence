The Problem

The signal lives between the sources.

The forces shaping Canadian industries are documented across government records, regulatory decisions, economic data, news reporting and across the web.

Fragmented across systems that were built to publish records, not produce intelligence. Understanding what changed, why it matters and where the impact may appear requires extensive manual research and interpretation.
